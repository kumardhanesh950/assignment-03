Types of numbers

  1. Natural numbers
    {1,2,3,4,5,...}
  2. Whole Numbers
    {0,1,2,3,4,....}
  3. Integers
    {...,-3,-2,-1,0,1,2,3,...}
  4. Even Numbers
    {0,2,4,6,...}
  5. Odd Numbers
    {1,3,5,7,...}

    Some important points:-

  • The Natural numbers are also called as Counting Numbers
  • 0 is even number
  • The some of rational and irrational numbers are called as Integers
